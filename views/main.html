THE BAND


The Dizzy Bangers are an Austin, Texas based rock and roll band, encompassing grunge, blues and hard psychedelic textures. Lyrics can be described as retrospective, dreamy and poetic, delivered by a haunting rock and roll voice.

The Dizzy Bangers formed in early 2012 when frontman Jimi Dharma began jamming and producing a handful of ideas with drummer Chad Goins. After turning Chad’s home office into a drum recording studio, Jimi moved to Costa Rica to focus on guitar, bass and vocal production. The two were able to remotely produce ‘Era of Heathen,’ their debut album in the summer of 2013.

Now stateside, current members are:

Jimi Dharma - Vocals/Guitar

RJM - Bass/Backing Vox

Kyle Vanderhoof - Drums

With a wealth of new material, The Dizzy Bangers are working on another record, expected for release 2020. Check out 5 new singles released on Spotify, and look out for show dates in central Texas and beyond.
